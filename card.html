Pug

Is one of the oldest dog breeds in the world

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length: 30 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 15 years
  • Origin: China

Known for being very sociable and gentle companion, they are always in good mood and are rarely aggressive. Having a cute way, they have the ability to cheer up their owners just staring at them or making a little effort.
